# Jack Remove
1. Press `Add`
2. Change `Action` to `Move`
	1. Copy `data` ID and paste it in `Service:` 
3. If text in the `Room` cell exists 
	1. In `Move To Location:` type in the building name (`CPA`) 
	2. Press Enter
	3. Then add a space and type in the room number (`D101B`) 
	4. Press Enter
	5. Double click on the only result
4. Then press `Save New` 
5. Click check box in the top left
6. Press `Save Without SDC`
7. Press `Cabling` tab
8. Double click on `In` Action 
9. Copy `Aruba SWX ` and paste it in `Origination Equipment:` 
10. Copy `Aruba Port` and paste it in `Origination Prt/Pin/Len:` 
11. Press `Save and Close` >> `Save and Close` >> `Save`
